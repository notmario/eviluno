This is a game based on the card game UNO. If you are not familiar with the rules of UNO, this is not the place for you.

Currently a work in progress.

The objective of the game is to have no cards in your hand. You win the game when this occurs. If multiple players have no cards in hand, then they do not win and the game continues. The deck contains 440 cards at present in 8 colours (red, orange, yellow, green, cyan, blue, purple, and magenta), with varying effects.

The player who last won a game of The Campaign for North Africa goes first.
In the unlikely event that nobody has completed a game of The Campaign for North Africa, a different method may be used to determine the starting player.

Deal out a seven card hand to each player and flip over the top two cards of the deck to form two play piles. There is also a discard pile which starts empty, and will be filled by card effects.

On your turn, you may play one card to a play pile, or draw a card. You must draw a card if you cannot play. You can play cards if they match the top card of the chosen play pile by colour or number, or if the played card is wild.
When you have only one card left in hand, you must call out "UNO", or any other word agreed upon by all players before the game. If you do not call it before someone else does, then you must draw two cards.
If you draw a card that is a valid playable card, you may play it. If the deck runs out of cards, shuffle all cards in the discard and play piles (not including the top card of each) to form the new deck.

If you draw a MOD card, you must immediately reveal it to all players, and then you must choose to either ACCEPT or DECLINE it.

ACCEPT: Draw four cards and put the MOD card in play in front of you.
DECLINE: Discard the MOD card and draw a replacement card.

You may have at most two MOD cards in front of you. If you would have more, choose MOD cards to discard until you have at most two.

Below is a list of all unique card types in the game, ignoring colour.
All wild cards can be played at any time on any pile. When played, choose a valid colour. The card's colour becomes the chosen colour.
When a card tells a player to draw cards (i.e. "the next player draws two cards"), their next turn is skipped if they are the next player, unless otherwise specified.
Additionally, all stackable cards may be played on top of all other stackable cards that match by either symbol or colour. A card is stackable if it features an arrow in the top right / bottom left corner. Cards are only drawn by the first player to not continue the stack.

2x col Numbers 0-10

0 and 10 only have one copy per colour.

There are also colourless numbers (depicted).
These can only be matched by their number, and not by colour.

2x col Reverse

Reverses the direction of play when played.

2x col Skip

The next player's turn is skipped.

2x col Draw Two

Stackable. The next player draws two cards.

16x Wild

Wild. Has no other effects.

4x Wild Draw Four

Stackable. Wild. The next player draws four cards.

1x col All Draw One

All other players draw one card. Their turns are not skipped.

2x Wild All Draw 2

Wild. All other players draw one card. Their turns are not skipped.

3x col #

When played, you may choose this card's number.

2x Wild x2

Stackable. Wild. The next player draws cards equal to the number of cards they have in hand.

When stacked, instead doubles the current stack.

2x Swap Hands

Wild. When played, choose a target player. You and that player swap your hands.

2x col Targeted Draw Two

Stackable. When played, choose a target player. That player draws two cards. If they stack on this, the turn order jumps to them.

2x Shuffle Hands

Wild. When played, shuffle all player's hands together and deal them out evenly, starting with you.

2x Wild Draw Eight

Stackable. Wild. The next player draws eight cards.

2x Wild =9

Wild. When played, target either you or the next player. That player draws or discards to exactly nine cards.

2x General Store

Wild. When played, flip over one card for each player. Starting with you, each player picks one of those cards to draw.

1x col Discard Two

When played, you must choose two cards to discard.

1x col Draw One

Stackable. The next player draws a card.

4x Wild Draw to Match

Wild. The next player draws cards until they draw a card of the chosen colour, or they have drawn eight cards.

2x Wild Divide By Two

Wild. When played, discard half of your hand, rounding up.

4x WAR

Wild. When played, each player discards a card. The players who discard the lowest numbered cards (cards without numbers count as 0) draw the cards split between them.

1x col Replay

When played, skip all other players.

1x 2cols Double Colour

Has no number, but two colours instead.

1x col Draw D6

Stackable. Roll a D6. The next player draws that many cards.

2x Wild 50/50

Stackable. Wild. When played, call a coin flip. If correct, the next player draws 10 cards. If stacked and failed, you draw the entire stack.

2x Trolley Problem

Wild. When played, flip 7 cards over and divide them into two piles (empty piles are allowed). The next player chooses one pile to draw.

2x Recycling

Wild. When played, discard your hand, then draw that many cards.

4x Wild Reverse

Wild. Reverses the direction of play when played.

4x Wild Skip

Wild. The next player's turn is skipped.

4x Lootbox

Wild. When played, flip six cards and draw three of them.

1x col Discard All X

When played, discard all cards in hand that have the same colour.

2x Parry

Stackable. Wild. When stacked, the last player who added to the stack draws the cards instead.

2x Dynamite

Can be played at any time. This is placed in front of you. At the start of your turn, discard the top card of the deck. If it is red, then you draw six and destroy this, else pass this to the player to your left.

1x col Draw Five

Stackable. The next player draws five cards.

1x Insult to Injury

MOD. Whenever you make a player draw cards, they draw one more.

1x Loud Yelling

MOD. You may pass your turn instead of playing.

1x Comically Large Spoon

MOD. You may draw four cards then discard two instead of playing.

1x Fishing Rod

MOD. You may draw the top card of a play pile instead of playing.

1x Red Card

MOD. Your red cards also skip the next player.

1x Magnifying Glass

MOD. Before your turn, you may look at the top card of the deck.

1x Shielding Potion

MOD. When you draw two or more cards, you may choose to draw one less card.

1x Glass Shard

MOD. When you draw cards or make another player draw cards, the amount is doubled.

1x Gambling Addiction

MOD. Before your turn, you may call a coin flip. If correct, take two turns where this does not trigger, else, skip your turn.

1x Sticker Sheet

MOD. You may destroy this card to repeat the effects of a card you play.

1x Push Your Temper

MOD. You may discard a card whenever your turn is skipped.

1x Energy Drink

MOD. You may destroy this. When you do, take extra turns until you cannot play a card. You are not allowed to play wild cards during these extra turns.

1x Mass Surveillance

MOD. You may draw a card and look at someone else's hand instead of playing.

1x Command Grab

MOD. You may destroy this to use it as a Wild Draw Four card.

1x Memory Loss

MOD. Before your turn, you may discard the top card of a play pile.

1x Tungsten Cube

MOD. You may use odd numbered cards as Wild cards, but you must draw two when you cannot play.

1x Fudge

MOD. You may use numbered cards as numbers 1 higher or lower.

1x Portal Gun

MOD. You treat blue and orange as the same colour, and purple and magenta as the same colour.

1x Immovable Object

MOD. Your turn cannot be skipped.

1x Marksman

MOD. When you play a basic "Draw N" or "Wild Draw N", you may draw a card to make it targeted. The card becomes unstackable.
